SAN REMIGIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL


San Remigio National High School is a school were students are very welcome even if they are different in race, lives in a different city or barangay.

SRNHS is also known in Cebu, other people who lives in other city said that SRNHS is a very good school because the students excels academically and even in extracurricular activities.

SRNHS also accepts STEM applicants every year.

Every year SRNHS celebrates different kinds of occasions by doing such activities, making booths, and having a fun program for all
